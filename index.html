Odena Essie Melton believed in cookies.

The cookie jar she kept in her cupboard was never empty. She made sure there was something in there to share with the family. Before I was born, the lid had been broken - but rather than buy a new jar, Maw-Maw put a bowl over the top to keep the cookies fresh. Waste not, want not!

I learned a lot from my grandmother before her passing, and after I received a book of my in-law's famliy recipes as a wedding present, I was thrilled to add those from my own family.

Using real ingredients and home-grown resourcefulness, our foremothers tested and perfected their recipes across decades and now - inspired by Maw-Maw's cookie jar - we're sharing them with you!
